# Smart Campus Event Management System - PRD ## 1. Student (The Participant) * **Goal:** Discoverability and ease of access. * **Key Features:** * **Personalized Dashboard:** "For You" feed based on department/interests. * **Smart Event Discovery:** Search and filters (Technical, Cultural, Sports, etc.). * **One-Click Registration:** Quick RSVP with secure payment integration for paid events. * **Digital Entry (QR Codes):** Unique QR code provided upon registration for check-in. * **Real-time Notifications:** Alerts for new events, schedule changes, and reminders. * **Feedback & Certification:** Post-event feedback and certificate downloads. ## 2. Organizer (The 3rd Party / Faculty / Club) * **Goal:** Management and execution. * **Key Features:** * **Event Proposal System:** Structured form for venue, crowd size, and budget. * **Venue & Resource Booking:** Live calendar for campus halls and equipment. * **Attendee Management:** Real-time list, exportable to CSV/PDF. * **QR Scanner Module:** Built-in scanner to verify tickets. * **Budget & Expense Tracker:** Log expenses and track revenue/sponsorships. * **Marketing Tools:** Banner/flyer uploads and broadcast messaging. ## 3. Admin (The Controller) * **Goal:** Governance and security. * **Key Features:** * **Approval Workflow:** Review/approve/reject event proposals with comments. * **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Manage organizer accounts and permissions. * **Campus-Wide Analytics:** Dashboard for engagement metrics and active departments. * **Conflict Resolution:** Master calendar flagging double-bookings. * **Database & System Logs:** Audit trails for accountability. * **Announcement Banner:** Global alerts for campus-wide news. ## 4. Core Technical "Smart" Features * **Conflict Prevention Logic:** Auto-prevention of double-bookings during submission. * **AI-Based Recommendations:** Suggestions based on attendance history. * **Automated Certificate Generation:** Auto-filled templates upon attendance verification.
